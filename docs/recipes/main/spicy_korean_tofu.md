# Korean Style Spicy Tofu

> *Crispy pan-fried tofu in a sweet and spicy gochujang sauce, garnished with sesame seeds and chives*

## Ingredients

- [ ] 500 g firm tofu
- [ ] 2 cloves garlic, minced
- [ ] ½ onion, finely chopped
- [ ] 2 cm fresh ginger, grated

### Sauce

- [ ] 3 tbsp gochujang
- [ ] 2 tbsp soy sauce or tamari
- [ ] 2 tbsp mirin
- [ ] 2 tsp brown sugar
- [ ] 225 ml water

### Garnish

- [ ] Sesame seeds
- [ ] Fresh chives, chopped

## Instructions

1. Preheat the oven to 200°C. Slice the tofu into rectangular pieces about 1 cm thick and place on a lined baking tray. Bake for 20 minutes to dry out the tofu.

2. Flip the tofu pieces and bake for another 10-15 minutes until golden brown.

3. While the tofu bakes, whisk together the gochujang, soy sauce, mirin, sugar, and water in a small bowl until smooth.

4. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add the garlic, onion, and ginger and cook until fragrant and lightly golden.

5. Add the baked tofu and pour the sauce over it. Simmer for 15 minutes, gently flipping the tofu halfway, until the sauce reduces and becomes sticky.

6. Serve garnished with sesame seeds and chopped chives.

## Recipe Notes

- Adjust gochujang amount based on spice preference: use 2 tbsp for milder, up to 4 tbsp for extra spicy
- Serve over steamed rice with vegetables like broccoli or green beans
