# Baja Fish Tacos

> *Crispy beer-battered cod tucked into corn tortillas and topped with slaw and a smoky chipotle sauce, Baja fish tacos are the perfect summer meal*

## Recipe Information

- **Total Time:** 40 minutes
- **Servings:** 4 servings
- **Course:** main
- **Difficulty:** medium
- **Language:** EN

## Ingredients

### Cabbage Slaw

- 250 g shredded red cabbage (about 5 cups)
- 3 tbsp minced red onion
- 15 g fresh chopped cilantro (about ½ cup)
- 3 tbsp cider vinegar
- 1½ tsp vegetable oil
- ½ tsp salt

### Chipotle Sauce

- 180 ml mayonnaise, best quality such as Hellmann's
- 2 tbsp lime juice, from one lime
- 2 to 3 chipotle chiles in adobo sauce, canned, roughly chopped, plus 1 to 2 tsp sauce
- 1 large clove garlic, roughly chopped

### Beer Batter

- 125 g all-purpose flour (about 1 cup)
- 1 tsp salt
- ½ tsp freshly ground black pepper
- 240 ml beer (about 1 cup)

### Fish and Tortillas

- 680 g skinless cod, cut into 2.5 cm wide x 10 cm long strips
- Vegetable oil (about 250 ml)
- 12 soft corn tortillas (15 cm)
- Lime wedges

## Instructions

### Cabbage Slaw

1. Toss the cabbage, red onion, cilantro, vinegar, oil and salt together in a medium bowl and set aside.

### Chipotle Sauce

1. Combine the mayonnaise, lime juice, chipotle chiles with sauce and garlic in a blender or mini food processor and blend until smooth. Set aside.

### Fish and Tortillas

1. Mix the flour, salt and pepper together in a medium bowl. Gradually add the beer, whisking until the batter is smooth with no lumps. Set aside.
2. In a medium skillet, over medium heat, add enough oil to reach a depth of 1.3 cm. Heat the oil until a deep-fry thermometer registers 175°C, or when the end of a wooden spoon sizzles when dipped into the oil.
3. Working in batches so as not to crowd the pan, dip the fish strips in the beer batter and coat on both sides. Let the excess batter drip off, then fry the fish in the hot oil until golden brown and cooked through, about 2 minutes per side. Transfer to a plate lined with paper towels to drain.
4. Smear each tortilla with a generous amount of the chipotle sauce, then top with the cabbage slaw. Lay one piece of fish inside each tortilla and serve with lime wedges.

## Recipe Notes

- 3 chipotle peppers and 2 tsp sauce will give you a spicy sauce. Use less for a milder sauce. You can freeze any leftover chipotle peppers from the can.
